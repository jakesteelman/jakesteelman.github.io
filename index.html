About Me

Hello! I'm Jake Steelman, a passionate, dedicated, and hardworking student entrepreneur currently attending the University of Central Oklahoma. I love all aspects of technology, but I'm particularly interested in programming, networking, and digital design. I currently own and operate Carbon Creative, a remote web design business that allows me to use my skills, expertise, and passion to help make a positive impact on local businesses. When I'm not working with technology, I like to work out in the gym, spend time with friends and family, learn new skills, and read.
